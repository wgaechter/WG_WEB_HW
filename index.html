Weather of the World!

This project collects and visualizes weather data from across the globe with the goal to finding trends relating to a city's latitude and its expected weather effects. We have data from across the globe with random cities to ensure a random sample for statistically relevent comparisons. We have completed visualizations for temperature, humidity, cloud cover, and wind speed.
